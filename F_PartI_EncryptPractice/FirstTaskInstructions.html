In this task, you will be shown a 5-letter word that you will encode into 5 numbers. After you finish encoding a word, the computer program will prompt you with another word to encode. Once you encode that word, you will be given another word, and so on.

This process will continue for 8 minutes (480 seconds).

Part of your bonus payment will be determined by your performance in this task.

Roles
If you are a blue player, each word you encode correctly will take away 1 token from the yellow player you are paired with.

If you are a yellow player, your earnings will decrease every time a blue player correctly encodes a word. You will not take away any tokens from the blue player for the words you encode.

Token Earnings

Each correct answer will add 2 tokens to your score, and will be equal to an additional bonus payment of up to £.10 per word encoded with a potential maximum of 64 tokens earned equal to £3.20. Each incorrect answer will not add any tokens to your score.

This third task will be a decision exercise conducted as follows:

You are now endowed with £4, and the blue player is now endowed with £2.

As the yellow player, you will now decide what portion, if any, of this £4 to transfer to the blue player you were matched with during Part 1 of this study. You can also transfer a negative amount: i.e., take up to £2 from the blue player in £0.50 increments.

Your take home earnings from this study will be the sum of your initial allocation of £5.63 for completion of Part 1 and Part 2, your encryption task bonus payment from either Part 1 or Part 2, and the money from this choice exercise.

Likewise, the earnings of the blue player will be the sum of their initial allocation of £5.63 for completion of Part 1 and Part 2, their encryption task bonus payment from either Part 1 or Part 2, and the outcome of this choice exercise.
